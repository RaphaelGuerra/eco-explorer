# Eco Explorer Goals

Last updated: 2026-01-19

- Offer multiple Atlantic Forest locations with distinct day/night visuals.
- Keep the core loop short, readable, and rewarding on desktop and mobile.
- Balance quick mini-games with lightweight progression (XP, streaks, perks).
- Introduce daily objectives and mission-based conservation tasks with clear progress and rewards.
- Make it easy to add new species, locations, and activities without refactors.
- Maintain clear, accessible UI copy and contrast.
